APOLLOCHAIN Token Distribution

Private Equity + ICO (40%):

the project plans to use 40% APL to complete its early financing for fulfilling most of the starting work of the project, including but not limited to: micro-grid pilot, micro-grid expansion test, smart metre subsequent R&D, construction of power station and grids, establishment of the trading platform, etc. The project is expected to use this 40% APL for a total of 3-round-financing covers Pre-ICO, Private Equity and ICO.

The project expects that the Pre-ICO fund will be mainly used for:

1. Various investigations and inspections in the early stage of the micro-grid pilot project, as well as finishing the on-site operation of the pilot micro-grid project. Before this, the APOLLOCHAIN Team will utilise this fund to conduct pre-investigative research on micro-grid projects in Australia. Specifically, the site selection for the pilot micro-grid project will be carried out in Australia, while the assessment will be based on workload, difficulty level, the degree of community cooperation, and the ease of construction, in order to finalise the optimal region for this pilot micro-grid project.

2. Execute the procurement of photovoltaic (PV) materials, construction and commissioning of PV power stations, infrastructure reconstruction, deployment of energy storage equipment and smart metres at the proposed pilot areas, for reaching the goal of conducting electricity trade in a sound environment.

3. Setting up the electricity trading platform, the R&D of 2nd-generation smart metre, and the development of mobile phone APPs that interact with our smart metres.

The fund raised by the Pre-ICO expects to be allocated as the followings:

5% - pilot site selection research and electricity retailer registration;
5% - development of the Electricity Exchange
5% - smart metre application and APP development
10% - construction and commissioning of the community micro-grid
10% - 2nd-generation smart metre development and community incentives
15% - marketing
20% - smart metre underlying AI training
20% - project team operations
10% - backup fund to enhance the project flexibility amongst each function
